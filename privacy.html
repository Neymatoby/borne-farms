Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

At Borne Farms, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when registering or using the platform, including:

  • Name, email address, and role
  • Farm and livestock data you enter
  • Wallet, investment, and transaction records
  • Voice notes, images, and other files you upload

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and operate the Borne Farms platform
  • Generate farm reports, analytics, and traceability records
  • Process transactions and co-ownership distributions
  • Communicate important updates about your account or farm

3. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device and on our backend servers. For the demo environment, all data is stored in a local SQLite database. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data when necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or with your explicit consent.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To request deletion, contact us at hello@bornefarms.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@bornefarms.com.
